Can anyone purchase a gift card without an account?
Very good question. As a security measure, customers must create an account (if they don‚Äôt already have one) prior to purchasing a gift card. This also means that if they lose track of the code for their gift card, they can log into their account to find it.
How does the gift card recipient get their code?
The person who purchased the gift card will see a Send button on the final screen after completing their purchase. This button opens up their email with a pre-populated message they can edit if they like before sending the Gift Card redemption code.
How can clients redeem online gift cards?
Clients can redeem online gift cards the same way as gift cards purchased administratively. Check out this guide on
Redeeming a Gift Card
for step-by-step instructions. üôÇ
Can clients redeem gift cards online?
Gift cards cannot be redeemed online when patients book appointments, and at this time there is no option to purchase products through the patient‚Äôs facing side. However, if the patient would like to redeem their gift card, they can provide the gift card code to the clinic, or the clinic can look up the patient‚Äôs profile and the gift cards under their billing.

Clients can see gift cards they‚Äôve purchased on their

Gift Cards

Jane tip: Gift cards purchased

What payment methods are accepted to purchase gift cards?
Because gift cards purchased online must be done through the Jane Payments integration, clients can pay by credit card. For online transactions, Jane Payments accepts Amex, Visa, Visa Debit, Mastercard, Debit Mastercard, Discover, Diner Club, and UnionPay.
My clinic has multiple Jane Payments accounts. Which one does the gift card sale get paid out to?
Excellent question! When a gift card is purchased, that money is deposited into the
last used
Jane Payments account. At the moment, Jane does not currently have the ability to choose which Jane Payments account the funds are deposited into.
If your clinic has multiple Jane Payments Accounts and you‚Äôd like to use our Online Gift Card feature, an option here would be to track those gift card sales so that you can manually compensate a practitioner if needed.

Can a gift card be reloaded online?
Not at this time, though gift cards can be reloaded administratively for a client.
Why would I want the option to set two different caps for the Gift Card load limit and Gift card online sale limit?
Jane has lots of safeguards in place, but whenever gift cards are available there is some risk of bad actors trying to exploit them. By allowing you to set different limits for Gift Card purchases made online and administratively, you have more control over that risk.
Ex. You could choose to allow Gift Cards to be sold/topped up in-person or over the phone for up to $2000, but reduce your Online Gift Card purchase limit to $200.
