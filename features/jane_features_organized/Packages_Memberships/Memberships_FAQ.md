‚≠ê I want to cancel a membership, but it‚Äôs only showing me the option to delete.

‚≠ê Can a patient pause their Membership and resume it at a later date?
Not at this time. A membership cannot be paused, however, you can cancel the membership and begin a new one at a later date, if needed.
