Can I receive inbound faxes?
Yes, though you will only be able to view them in Documo, not in Jane. There are a few spots in Documo where you can view incoming faxes, but the main one is the
History
page. Notifications for inbound faxes can also be sent directly to a user‚Äôs email.
Additionally, you can download an incoming fax and upload it to a patient‚Äôs chart as needed.
Do I need to create a user in Documo for each staff member using this integration?
We recommend creating separate Users in Documo for each staff member who will be logging into the Documo portal outside of Jane. That way, multiple people don‚Äôt share one Documo login.
You don‚Äôt need to log into the Documo portal to send a fax, only to view inbound faxes and your fax history (including a fax‚Äôs status).
Will I receive a notification for inbound faxes?
Each User in Documo can subscribe to the fax numbers they‚Äôd like to receive notifications for. When an inbound fax is sent to a number they‚Äôre subscribed to, they will receive an email notification.
To learn more about email notifications,
check out Documo‚Äôs guide here
.
Can each user in Documo be linked to a staff profile in Jane?
No, only one User in Documo can be linked to Jane at a time. The User you link to Jane is the one whose API key you choose when connecting Documo to Jane.
All faxes sent from Jane, regardless of which practitioner sends them, will come from one User in Documo and the fax number chosen as the account default.
What number do outbound faxes in Jane get sent from?
Whichever number is set as the account default will be the one that all faxes come from, regardless of which staff profile in Jane sends a fax through the integration.
You can edit the number chosen as the account default in Documo by heading to
Fax > My Fax Numbers > Set Primary
.
Can all users in Documo view outbound faxes?
Since all outbound faxes will come from the same default number, they will all show up in the fax history of the User profile in Documo that is connected to Jane.
Other Users in Documo will not be able to view those outbound faxes unless their User Role in Documo is set to Admin or Owner.
An Admin can view all inbound and outbound faxes for all other users in Documo, and an Owner can view all faxes plus the Billing tab. To learn more about User permission levels in Documo,
check out their guide
.
To change a User‚Äôs role in Documo, head to Users and click the cogwheel next to their profile.
From here, you can click the
User Role

Don‚Äôt forget to hit the

Here are some quick tips from Documo:
6100 error code is a busy signal and also the most common error.
8000‚Äôs error codes usually mean a connection failure from either the sender or recipient. Always try resending these faxes, and remember, failed faxes do not count towards your license limit of 500 pages per month.
5000‚Äôs error codes refer to an issue with the document being faxed. You can reach out to Documo at support@documo.com for assistance.

What if I send & receive way more than the 500-page per month limit?
If you send and receive far more than 500 pages a month or require more than 3 fax numbers/users, please email
jane@documo.com
to request a custom rate.
Do inbound faxes count towards my 500-page per month limit?
Yes, any pages that are successfully transmitted count toward your monthly total, including inbound pages.
Are outbound cover pages counted towards my 500-page per month limit?
Yes, outbound cover pages are included in your 500-page monthly limit. Every successfully sent page, including cover pages, counts toward your total.
Do failed faxes count towards my 500-page per month limit?
No, only pages that are successfully transmitted count towards your monthly total.
How can I track my page count to see if I‚Äôm close or over 500 pages?
There isn‚Äôt a notification that lets you know when you‚Äôve reached 500 pages a month, so you‚Äôll want to keep an eye on this by heading to the

How do I transfer over my existing fax number?
You can transfer over your existing fax number for a one-time fee of $15 USD. Submit a port request through the porting tab in Documo to get started. It can take between 7-10 business days for your request to be completed. Check out

If you are currently signed up for Documo‚Äôs 2-week trial, we recommend you wait to transfer over your existing fax number until you have signed up for a paid plan to ensure there‚Äôs no interruption for inbound faxes.
If you have any questions or concerns regarding the porting process feel free to reach out to the Documo team via email at

Documo has an address book that you can import contacts to by uploading a CSV file. Check out

Will Jane use my contacts in Documo when sending a fax?
No, Jane doesn‚Äôt pull your contacts from Documo. However, if you‚Äôve faxed a contact recently, Jane will auto-complete their information when you begin typing their name into the

Can I customize my cover sheet details based on patient and practitioner?
The cover page template set as the Account Default in Documo will automatically be used for all faxes, but you can change it anytime in the Documo portal.
When sending a fax from Jane, you can also add customized details into the
Subject
and
Notes

Can each practitioner send a fax from their own fax number?
The Documo plan for Jane customers includes 3 fax numbers, but only one can be set as the default at a time. The default number is the one faxes in Jane will be sent from, and the one that replies will be sent to.

Can I receive inbound faxes?
Yes, though you will only be able to view them in Documo, not in Jane. There are a few spots in Documo where you can view incoming faxes, but the main one is the
History
page. Notifications for inbound faxes can also be sent directly to a user‚Äôs email.
Additionally, you can download an incoming fax and upload it to a patient‚Äôs chart as needed.
Do I need to create a user in Documo for each staff member using this integration?
We recommend creating separate Users in Documo for each staff member who will be logging into the Documo portal outside of Jane. That way, multiple people don‚Äôt share one Documo login.
You don‚Äôt need to log into the Documo portal to send a fax, only to view inbound faxes and your fax history (including a fax‚Äôs status).
Will I receive a notification for inbound faxes?
Each User in Documo can subscribe to the fax numbers they‚Äôd like to receive notifications for. When an inbound fax is sent to a number they‚Äôre subscribed to, they will receive an email notification.
To learn more about email notifications,
check out Documo‚Äôs guide here

Can each user in Documo be linked to a staff profile in Jane?
No, only one User in Documo can be linked to Jane at a time. The User you link to Jane is the one whose API key you choose when connecting Documo to Jane.
All faxes sent from Jane, regardless of which practitioner sends them, will come from one User in Documo and the fax number chosen as the account default.
What number do outbound faxes in Jane get sent from?
Whichever number is set as the account default will be the one that all faxes come from, regardless of which staff profile in Jane sends a fax through the integration.
You can edit the number chosen as the account default in Documo by heading to
Fax > My Fax Numbers > Set Primary
.
Can all users in Documo view outbound faxes?
Since all outbound faxes will come from the same default number, they will all show up in the fax history of the User profile in Documo that is connected to Jane.
Other Users in Documo will not be able to view those outbound faxes unless their User Role in Documo is set to Admin or Owner.
An Admin can view all inbound and outbound faxes for all other users in Documo, and an Owner can view all faxes plus the Billing tab. To learn more about User permission levels in Documo,
check out their guide
.
To change a User‚Äôs role in Documo, head to Users and click the cogwheel next to their profile.
From here, you can click the

Admin

Don‚Äôt forget to hit the

8000‚Äôs error codes usually mean a connection failure from either the sender or recipient. Always try resending these faxes, and remember, failed faxes do not count towards your license limit of 500 pages per month.

If you send and receive far more than 500 pages a month or require more than 3 fax numbers/users, please email

Do inbound faxes count towards my 500-page per month limit?
Yes, any pages that are successfully transmitted count toward your monthly total, including inbound pages.

Yes, outbound cover pages are included in your 500-page monthly limit. Every successfully sent page, including cover pages, counts toward your total.
Do failed faxes count towards my 500-page per month limit?
No, only pages that are successfully transmitted count towards your monthly total.
