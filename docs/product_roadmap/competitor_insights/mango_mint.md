## Overview

Mango Mint is a comprehensive business management platform with 363 documented features across 19 major categories. They focus heavily on automation, SMS-based workflows, and operational efficiency.

**Total Features Analyzed**: 44 core features
**Our Feature Parity**: 34%

---

## Key Differentiators

### 1. SMS-First Automation
- Express Booking™ via SMS links
- Virtual waiting room check-in
- Automated waitlist notifications
- Self check-in/checkout via text

**Why It Matters**: Reduces staff workload, improves patient experience

### 2. Resource Management
- Prevents double-booking of rooms/equipment
- Critical for laser clinics with limited machines
- Visual conflict detection

**Why It Matters**: Core operational efficiency for med spas

### 3. Processing Time Optimization
- Multi-phase appointment timing (numbing, treatment, recovery)
- Allows practitioners to see multiple patients during wait times
- Maximizes revenue per hour

**Why It Matters**: Perfect for injectables workflow (Botox, fillers)

### 4. Intelligent Waitlist
- Automatic matching of cancellations to waitlist
- SMS-based first-come-first-served booking
- Revenue recovery system

**Why It Matters**: Reduces revenue loss from no-shows

### 5. HIPAA Compliance Framework
- Built-in compliance tools
- BAA agreements
- PHI handling best practices

**Why It Matters**: Legal requirement for medical procedures
