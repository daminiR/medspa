‚öñÔ∏è Compliance: 10DLC & TCPA Requirements

‚ö†Ô∏è CRITICAL: As of February 2025, 10DLC Registration Required

Messages without 10DLC registration may be BLOCKED by carriers. Registration costs $4/brand + $2-10/mo per campaign via Twilio. TCPA violations carry penalties of $500-$1,500 PER MESSAGE with no damage cap for class actions.

Message Type Consent Required Examples
Transactional Prior express consent (verbal OK) Appointment reminders, confirmations, cancellations
Marketing Prior express WRITTEN consent Promotions, specials, upsells, new service announcements

üö® Required Platform Features

  • Separate consent tracking (transactional vs marketing)
  • Automatic opt-out keyword handling (STOP, END, CANCEL)
  • 8 AM - 9 PM time zone enforcement
  • Audit trail of all consent with timestamps
  • 10-day max opt-out processing
  • Business name in every message

‚úÖ 10DLC Registration Steps

  • Register brand with Tax ID via Twilio ($4)
  • Register campaigns by use case ($2-10/mo each)
  • Provide business address and functional website
  • 4-day approval process
