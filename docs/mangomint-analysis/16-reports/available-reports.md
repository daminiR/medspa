This article covers the reports available in the Reports app.

To access reports, you must be an Admin user, or you must have the Can access company reports permission enabled in Staff Member settings.

Link copied
Staff Reports

Learn more about each Staff report listed below.

Report

	

Description




Payroll

	

Includes payroll information for staff members. Learn more about this report.




Service & Product Sales By Staff

	

Includes quantities and sales totals for services and products sold by each staff member.




Time Clock

	

Includes information on staff member working hours. This report is available if the Time Clock app is enabled.




Days Off

	

Includes days off for staff members during the selected time period and for the selected locations.

Report

	

Description




Sales Summary

	

Includes quantities and sales totals of services and products for each day.




Service Sales

	

Includes quantities and sales totals of services.




Product Sales

	

Includes quantities and sales totals of products.




Sales by Time Period

	

Includes service and product sales totals grouped by day, week, or month for the selected time period and locations.

Payment Reports

Learn more about each Payment report listed below.

Report

	

Description




Payment Summary

	

Includes quantities and totals of payments by payment method. Also includes membership usage, package usage, reversal payments, and credit card refunds.




Payment Details

	

Includes payment details for each sale such as payment method and payment amount, including reversal payments and credit card refunds.




Cash Drawer Activity

	

Includes all cash payments, reversed cash payments, cash drawer pay-ins/pay-outs, and manual adjustments to the cash drawer. This report is available if the Cash Drawer app is enabled.




Deposits Collected

	

Includes deposits collected via online booking or Express Booking™.




Deposits Used

	

Includes used deposits based on their associated sale date.

Business Reports

Learn more about each Business report listed below.

Report

	

Description




Cashflow

	

Includes gross and net totals for cashflow. Includes all cash-equivalent forms of payment including cash, checks, and credit cards. This report ignores non-cash payments such as gift cards and packages.




Business Intelligence: Appointments

	

Provides insight into appointment metrics such as booked percentage (productivity), pre-bookings, walk-ins, and staff requests.




Business Intelligence: Sales

	

Provides insight into sales metrics such as average product total per sale for each staff member.




Business Intelligence: Forecast

	

Provides insight into future business metrics such as productivity and appointments booked.




Client Retention

	

Specifies how many clients from the selected time period visited again within 90 or 180 days.




Appointment Cancellations

	

Includes client and appointment details for canceled appointments, deleted appointments, and expired Express Bookings™.
