## Competitive Strengths by Platform

### Zenoti - Enterprise AI Leader
**Best For:** Large multi-location chains with big budgets
**Strengths:**
- Zeenie AI (content generation, multilingual, review responses)
- Smart campaigns (auto-optimization)
- Advanced marketing analytics
- Revenue attribution

**Weaknesses:**
- Expensive, no pricing transparency
- No free trial
- No real-time conversation AI
- Complex UI

---

### Mangomint - Modern UX Champion
**Best For:** Small-medium teams wanting great automation
**Strengths:**
- Best automation flows (12+ triggers, conditional logic)
- Mangomint Connect (phone + text + chat)
- Excellent UX
- Goal tracking

**Weaknesses:**
- Two-way texting costs extra $75/mo
- No AI features
- Credits system can be confusing
- Not for solo practitioners

---

### Boulevard - Premium Experience
**Best For:** Luxury salons prioritizing client experience
**Strengths:**
- Contact Center (phone + text unified)
- Smart reminder timing
- Premium brand positioning
- Behavioral learning

**Weaknesses:**
- Most expensive
- Per-message fees (user complaints)
- 12-month contract required
- Limited automation vs. competitors

---

### Jane App - Healthcare Simplicity
**Best For:** Solo healthcare practitioners
**Strengths:**
- Strong HIPAA/consent management
- Secure in-app messaging
- Client preference control
- Simple, clean UX

**Weaknesses:**
- Messaging still in Beta
- 150-character SMS limit
- No attachments
- Limited campaign features
- SMS only on higher plans

---

### Vagaro - Budget-Friendly
**Best For:** Cost-conscious small businesses
**Strengths:**
- Most affordable ($20/mo entry)
- Good automation variety (4 types)
- Embedded links, tracking
- Toll-free number included

**Weaknesses:**
- Basic two-way texting
- No AI capabilities
- Toll-free less engaging than local
- Limited advanced features

---

### Pabau - Med Spa Specialist
**Best For:** Medical aesthetics practices
**Strengths:**
- HIPAA/GDPR compliant
- Pre/post-care automation
- Medical-specific triggers (Botox recalls)
- Payment/results via SMS

**Weaknesses:**
- Limited public documentation
- No AI features
- Custom pricing (unclear)
- Not differentiated enough
