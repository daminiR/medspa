# Competitive Messaging/Texting Pricing Research
## Medical Spa & Salon Management Software - 2026

**Research Date:** January 9, 2026
**Focus:** SMS/Text messaging pricing across major medical spa management platforms

---

## Executive Summary

This research analyzes messaging/texting pricing across 7 major medical spa and salon management platforms plus direct Twilio costs. Key findings:

- **Most Competitive:** Jane App (unlimited SMS included in base plan)
- **Most Expensive:** Mangomint ($75/mo + $25/location for unlimited)
- **Most Transparent:** Boulevard (clear tiered pricing)
- **Direct Cost Baseline:** Twilio ($0.0079 per SMS segment)

### Pricing Models Identified:
1. **Included/Unlimited** - Jane App
2. **Tiered Usage** - Boulevard
3. **Credit-Based** - Mangomint, Zenoti
4. **Fixed Add-On** - Vagaro, Mangomint Connect
5. **Per-User Allowance** - Aesthetic Record
6. **Contact for Quote** - PatientNow

---

## Detailed Competitive Analysis

### 1. Mangomint
**Model:** Dual pricing (Credit-based marketing + Unlimited two-way texting)

#### Mangomint Connect (Two-Way Texting)
- **Price:** $75/month (first location) + $25/month per additional location
- **Features:** Unlimited messaging, dedicated phone number per location
- **Includes:** Two-way texting, web chat, phone calls
- **Upgrade:** Automatic upgrade from Two-Way Texting at no additional cost

#### Flow Marketing Messages (Credit-Based)
- **Credit Cost:** $0.01 per credit (à la carte) or <$0.01 with credit plan
- **Credit Plans:** Available with monthly subscription (exact prices not publicly listed)
- **Text Message Cost:**
  - Base: 10 credits per SMS (≈$0.10)
  - Increases by 5 credits per additional segment
  - With images: +20 credits (≈$0.20)
  - Merge tags: Count as 15 characters each
- **Email Cost:** 5 credits per email (≈$0.05)
- **Rollover:** À la carte credits roll over; plan credits reset monthly

#### Total Monthly Cost Examples:
- **1 location, unlimited texting:** $75/month
- **3 locations, unlimited texting:** $75 + $50 = $125/month
- **10 locations, unlimited texting:** $75 + $225 = $300/month

**Strengths:**
- Unlimited two-way texting option
- Clear per-location pricing
- Marketing automation with credits

**Weaknesses:**
- Most expensive base price for unlimited messaging
- Credit-based marketing adds complexity
- Per-location fees add up quickly

### 2. Boulevard
**Model:** Tiered usage pricing per location

#### Pricing Tiers (Monthly)
Based on total sent + received segments per location:

| Segments/Month | Monthly Cost | Cost Per Message (Avg) |
|----------------|--------------|------------------------|
| 1-500          | $25          | $0.05                  |
| 501-1,000      | $40          | $0.04                  |
| 1,001-2,000    | $60          | $0.03                  |
| 2,001-3,000    | $80          | $0.027                 |
| 3,001-5,000    | $120         | $0.024                 |
| 5,001-7,000    | $150         | $0.021                 |
| 7,001-10,000   | $200         | $0.02                  |
| 10,000+        | +$50/5K      | $0.01-0.02             |

#### Free Text Allowances by Plan:
- **Essentials Plan:** 100 free texts/month
- **Aesthetics Starter:** 250 free texts/month
- **Aesthetics Bundle:** 2,500 free texts/month

#### Text Segment Details:
- **SMS:** 160 characters per segment (70 with emoji/unicode)
- **MMS:** 1,600 characters + image = 3 segments
- **Billing:** Both sent AND received segments count toward usage
- **Features Included:** Two-way texting, client notifications, text blasts

#### Cost Examples:
- **500 messages/month:** $25/month (with 100 free = 400 billable → Tier 1)
- **1,500 messages/month:** $60/month (with 250 free = 1,250 billable → Tier 3)
- **5,000 messages/month:** $150/month (with 2,500 free = 2,500 billable → Tier 4)

**Calculation for Scenarios:**
- **1 location, 500 msgs:** 500 - 100 free = 400 segments → $25/month
- **3 locations, 1,500 msgs:** (500 each) - 300 free = 1,200 segments → $60/month per location = $180/month total
- **10 locations, 5,000 msgs:** (500 each) - 1,000 free = 4,000 segments → $120/month per location = $1,200/month total

**Strengths:**
- Transparent tiered pricing
- Clear segment definitions
- Scales with usage
- Free allowances by plan tier

**Weaknesses:**
- Per-location pricing adds up
- Received messages count toward usage
- Can be expensive for high-volume users

### 3. Zenoti
**Model:** Credit-based consumption with custom pricing

#### Pricing Structure:
- **Credits:** Purchase via Zenoti Marketplace (one-time or monthly recurring)
- **Cost Per Credit:** Custom per account (varies by country/region)
- **Supported Currencies:** USD, GBP, AUD, CAD
- **Pricing Levels:**
  1. Global-level pricing (base)
  2. Country-level pricing (regional override)
  3. Account-level pricing (custom contracts)

#### Sample Rates (India, August 2025 - for reference):
- 10,000 credits, 6 months validity: Rs. 4,000 + taxes (≈$0.05/credit USD equivalent)
- 50,000 credits, 12 months validity: Rs. 17,500 + taxes (≈$0.04/credit USD equivalent)

#### Features:
- SMS, email, and voice included in subscription
- Usage billed based on consumption
- Optional base packs available
- Automatic or manual recharge
- One-time purchases available instantly
- Recurring credits may take 1 business day

#### Cost Estimation (Using India rates as proxy):
**Note:** US rates are likely higher; contact Zenoti for exact pricing

- **500 messages/month:** ≈500-1,000 credits ≈ $25-50/month (estimated)
- **1,500 messages/month:** ≈1,500-3,000 credits ≈ $75-150/month (estimated)
- **5,000 messages/month:** ≈5,000-10,000 credits ≈ $250-500/month (estimated)

**Strengths:**
- Flexible credit-based system
- One-time or recurring options
- Credits for SMS, email, and voice
- Custom pricing per account

**Weaknesses:**
- Pricing not transparent
- Varies by region and contract
- Requires contacting sales for exact costs

#### Features:

#### Cost Examples:

**Strengths:**

### 5. Jane App
**Model:** Unlimited SMS included in base plans

#### Pricing:
- **Practice Plan:** $79/month base + $35/full-time practitioner
- **Thrive Plan:** $399/month (pricing varies by practitioners)
- **SMS Reminders:** Unlimited, included at NO additional cost
- **Availability:** Select regions (varies by location)

#### Features:
- Unlimited automated SMS appointment reminders
- Email reminders on all plans
- SMS only on Legacy, Practice, or Thrive plans
- Secure messaging via mobile app (uses push notifications)
- Third-party provider: Twilio
- Local/toll-free numbers by region

#### Cost to Jane:
- $1.7 Million USD spent on SMS in 2024 for all customers
- Jane absorbs the cost instead of passing to customers
- Strategy: Push notifications for new messaging features to avoid SMS costs

#### Cost Examples (Practice Plan):
- **1 location, 1 practitioner:** $79 + $35 = $114/month (unlimited SMS)
- **3 practitioners:** $79 + ($35 × 3) = $184/month (unlimited SMS)
- **10 practitioners:** $79 + ($35 × 10) = $429/month (unlimited SMS)

**Strengths:**
- Unlimited SMS reminders included
- No per-message costs
- Predictable pricing
- Customer-friendly model
- No SMS credit management

**Weaknesses:**
- SMS only on higher-tier plans
- Regional availability varies
- Must pay per-practitioner even for unlimited SMS

### 8. Twilio (Direct Integration)
**Model:** Pay-as-you-go per message

#### SMS Pricing (United States):
- **Outbound SMS:** $0.0079 per message segment
- **Inbound SMS:** $0.0079 per message segment
- **Outbound MMS:** $0.0200 per message
- **Inbound MMS:** $0.0100 per message

#### Message Segments:
- **Standard SMS:** 160 characters
- **Unicode/Emoji:** 70 characters per segment
- **MMS:** Up to 1,600 characters + media

#### Additional Costs:
- **Phone Numbers:** Varies by type (local, toll-free, short code)
  - Local: ≈$1.15/month
  - Toll-free: ≈$2.00/month
- **Carrier Fees:** Automatically applied per message
- **Failed Messages:** $0.001 per failed message
- **A2P 10DLC Registration:** One-time and ongoing fees

#### Volume Discounts:
- Automatically applied as usage scales
- Cannot combine phone number types for discounts
- Calculated per phone number type

#### Cost Examples (SMS only, excluding phone number fees):
- **500 messages/month:** 500 × $0.0079 = $3.95/month
- **1,500 messages/month:** 1,500 × $0.0079 = $11.85/month
- **5,000 messages/month:** 5,000 × $0.0079 = $39.50/month

#### Multiple Locations (with phone numbers):
- **1 location:** $3.95 + $2 = $5.95/month (500 msgs)
- **3 locations:** $11.85 + $6 = $17.85/month (1,500 msgs)
- **10 locations:** $39.50 + $20 = $59.50/month (5,000 msgs)

**Strengths:**
- Lowest per-message cost
- Transparent pricing
- Pay only for what you use
- Volume discounts available
- No contracts required
- Most flexible

**Weaknesses:**
- Requires technical integration
- No user interface included
- Must build messaging features
- Carrier fees add complexity
- A2P registration required
- No customer support tools

## Recommendations for Our Platform

### Competitive Positioning Options:

#### Option 1: Jane App Model (Most Customer-Friendly)
- Include unlimited SMS in base subscription
- Absorb Twilio costs
- Differentiate on customer experience
- **Pro:** Best customer satisfaction, simplest
- **Con:** Highest cost to us, risk with high-volume users

#### Option 2: Boulevard Model (Most Transparent)
- Implement clear tiered pricing
- Start with free allowance (500-1,000 messages)
- Charge $25-200 based on usage tiers
- **Pro:** Fair, scales with usage, transparent
- **Con:** Requires usage tracking, billing complexity

#### Option 3: Aesthetic Record Model (Most Balanced)
- Charge per-user with included allowance
- 200-500 SMS per user per month
- Simple to scale with team growth
- **Pro:** Predictable, scales naturally
- **Con:** Users may not fully utilize allowance

#### Option 4: Hybrid Model (Recommended)
- **Tier 1 (Starter):** 500 free SMS/month included in base plan
- **Tier 2 (Growth):** 2,000 free SMS/month at +$25/month
- **Tier 3 (Professional):** 5,000 free SMS/month at +$50/month
- **Tier 4 (Enterprise):** Unlimited SMS at +$75/month per location
- **Overage:** $0.02 per message over allowance (2.5x Twilio cost)

### Pricing Strategy Recommendations:

1. **Start Competitive:** Match or beat Jane App value
2. **Be Transparent:** Clear pricing like Boulevard
3. **Scale Fairly:** Don't penalize growth
4. **Bundle Smart:** Include base allowance in all plans
5. **Markup Reasonably:** 2-3x Twilio costs for sustainability

### Feature Differentiation:

Instead of competing on price alone, differentiate on:
1. **AI-Powered Messaging:** Smart templates, auto-responses
2. **HIPAA Compliance:** Built-in compliance tools
3. **Two-Way Conversations:** Rich conversation history
4. **Smart Scheduling:** AI-driven appointment reminders
5. **Analytics:** Message effectiveness tracking
6. **Multi-Channel:** SMS + Email + Push + In-App
7. **Automation:** Workflow-based messaging
8. **Personalization:** Advanced tokenization and merge fields
